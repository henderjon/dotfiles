700 (drwx------) - .ssh directory
644 (-rw-r--r--) - public keys (.pub files)
644 (-rw-r--r--) - known_hosts
600 (-rw-------) - private keys (id_rsa)
600 (-rw-------) - authorized_keys
600 (-rw-------) - config

$USER home directory should not be writeable by the group or others (at most 755 (drwxr-xr-x)).
